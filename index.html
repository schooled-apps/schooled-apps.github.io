Why an umbrella

One institution. One team. Many apps.

The bursar's tools differ from the housing officer's, which differ from the bus coordinator's. We just make sure they all talk.

One directory

Add a student in Finance, the bus driver sees them in Safirikids the next morning, the warden sees them in Bweni at check-in.

One bill

All the products on a single invoice. No three vendors to chase at term-end.

One team to call

Same number for the M-Pesa question, the hostel double-booking, the bus running late. We sort it out internally.

Built around the year

Terms, semesters, exam weeks, fee deadlines. Whether you run three terms or two semesters, the software follows the calendar.

See it on your school.

A short call usually tells both sides whether we are a fit. We do private demos against your fee structure, your bus routes, your school calendar.
