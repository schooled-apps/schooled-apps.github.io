What it does

Built around how schools and campuses actually run.

Built in minutes

Tell Schooled your subjects, periods per week, teachers, and venues. The timetable assembles itself — no more three-day pen-and-paper sessions.

Streams or sections

Single-stream secondary, multi-stream high school, university sections — each gets its own conflict-free schedule, sharing teachers, labs, and lecture halls cleanly.

Double periods

Sciences, languages, lectures, exam prep — request double periods or two-hour blocks and Schooled keeps them adjacent.

Venues that matter

Labs, lecture halls, computer rooms, the music room. Tag each venue's purpose; only the right subjects get scheduled there.

Teacher and lecturer load

Set max lessons per day and per week per staff member. Schooled honours it — no one locked into seven straight periods.

Constraints that bend

Some staff leave at noon Wednesdays. Chapel is fixed Thursday morning. The dean has Friday afternoons off. Schooled treats those as hard rules and works around them.

How it works

Three steps, one afternoon.

Step 01

Tell us your institution

Subjects, classes or sections, teachers or lecturers, venues, periods per day. Setup in one afternoon, with one of our team on a call.

Step 02

Press generate

Schooled solves the puzzle. Hard constraints honoured (chapel, venue availability, staff off-days). Soft preferences balanced (no seven-period days).

Step 03

Print and start the term

Per-class, per-teacher, per-lecturer, per-venue printouts. Plus the master grid. Post, share, hand out. Done.

Who uses Timetable

Built for the people who run the term.

Deputy / dean / scheduler

Day-to-day operator. Builds the master grid; handles mid-term changes when staff are absent.

Heads of department

Set how many periods each subject needs. Approve venue rotation. Override clashes when staff trade.

Class teachers

See their class's full week on one screen. Print copies for the register.

Subject staff / lecturers

Their personal week — every class, every venue, every block — with reminders the morning of.

Works with the rest of Schooled

Teachers and students set up in Schooled Finance appear in Timetable automatically. No re-entry, no spreadsheet imports between products.

Want to see it on your institution's structure?

Send us your subjects, your streams or sections, and your current timetable. We come back with a worked example using your structure.
