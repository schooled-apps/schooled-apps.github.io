Schooled Timetable

Class timetables that build themselves. Set your subjects, periods, teachers, and labs — Schooled handles the puzzle and you start the term with the printout in hand.

Safirikids

Live bus tracking, verified pick-ups and drop-offs, and a calm channel between drivers, schools, and parents. Replaces the school-transport WhatsApp group with something both calmer and auditable.

Lives on its own brand and its own site. Students added in Schooled Finance are visible to drivers in Safirikids the next morning.
